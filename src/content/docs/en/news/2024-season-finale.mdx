The final event of the 2024 season closed on a strong note for the team.

**Published: December 2024**

---

## Event Recap

At the last competition of the 2024 season, the team delivered one of its strongest overall performances to date. The result reflected a full year of technical iteration, logistics work, and steady execution by members across every subsystem.

### Results

- **Static events:** strong reviews in design, cost, and business presentation
- **Dynamic events:** the team completed acceleration, skidpad, high-speed obstacle avoidance, and endurance-related runs
- **Overall ranking:** the team achieved its best result so far

## Season Highlights

### Technical progress

1. **Perception upgrade:** a new LiDAR-and-camera fusion pipeline improved environment awareness.
2. **Planning and control refinement:** faster response and more stable trajectory tracking.
3. **Higher system reliability:** the full stack held up under repeated competition pressure.

### Team growth

Throughout the season, members moved from training-stage uncertainty to far stronger execution in their own roles:

- the algorithm group solved several difficult technical issues
- the electrical group maintained system stability and integration
- the mechanical group kept the car reliable and race-ready
- the operations group coordinated logistics, documents, and support work

## Acknowledgments

The season result belongs to the whole team. It also reflects the support of advisors, sponsors, and alumni who kept the project moving.

### Special thanks

- Hubei University of Automotive Technology
- School of Automotive Engineers
- sponsor partners and supporters
- alumni from previous seasons

---

## Looking Ahead

The 2024 season is complete, but the development cycle is not. Work on the next-generation car has already started, and the team will carry the lessons of 2024 into the 2025 season.

> Strive upward. Drive the future.
