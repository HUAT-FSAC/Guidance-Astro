## Technical Direction

### Perception system

The next-generation perception stack will move toward deeper multi-sensor fusion:

```
LiDAR ----> point-cloud fusion ----> 3D detection --
                                                   |
Camera ---> image fusion -------> semantic cues ---+--> fused perception output
```

**Key technical points**
- time synchronization through hardware triggering and precise clock alignment
- spatial calibration with a more automated extrinsic-calibration workflow
- fusion algorithms that can better combine geometric and semantic information

### Localization and mapping

<Aside type="tip">
  The team plans to explore vision-dominant localization strategies for scenarios where GNSS is unreliable or unavailable.
</Aside>

- graph-based SLAM with factor-graph fusion
- loop closure that combines visual and geometric cues
- incremental map maintenance and update strategies

### Planning and control

**Planning layer**
- global planning from track structure to drivable path
- local planning for real-time obstacle avoidance and path smoothing

**Control layer**
- longitudinal control through speed planning and tracking
- lateral control through Stanley, Pure Pursuit, and MPC-style methods

## Recruiting

The new season needs more members who want to build real systems and learn fast.

### Open directions

| Group | Focus | Baseline skills |
|------|----------|----------|
| Sensing | point-cloud processing, detection | C++/Python, PCL, PyTorch |
| Localization | SLAM, sensor fusion | C++, ROS, Kalman filtering |
| Planning & Control | path planning, vehicle control | Python/C++, control theory |
| Simulation | simulation tooling | ROS, Gazebo, Carla |

### How to join

1. Read the [learning roadmap](/en/archive/2024/2024-learning-roadmap/)
