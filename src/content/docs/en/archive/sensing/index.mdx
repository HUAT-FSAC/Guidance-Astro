## Overview

<div class="image-grid">

![cam-and-camera-fusion](@assets/docs/archive/sensing/sensing-and-fusion/cam-and-camera-fusion.jpg)

![actual-and-virtual](@assets/docs/archive/sensing/sensing-and-fusion/actual-and-virtual.png)

</div>

The sensing group uses cameras, LiDAR, and related perception hardware to estimate the position, type, and color of obstacles around the race car, then feeds the processed results to the rest of the autonomous stack.

<Aside type="tip" title="Why It Matters">
    Perception is the foundation of the full driverless pipeline. Without stable sensing output, downstream planning and control cannot work reliably.
</Aside>
