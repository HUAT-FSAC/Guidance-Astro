## Project Management

### Progress tracking

Use project-management tools to track vehicle design, manufacturing, and test progress:

| Tool | Strength | Recommended use |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| Feishu Projects | Localized and free | Daily collaboration |
| Notion | Flexible and powerful | Knowledge management |
| Jira | Professional workflow control | Complex projects |
| Gantt chart | Clear visual timeline | Milestone reporting |

### Milestone planning

<Steps>
1. **Design phase** - September to December
2. **Manufacturing phase** - January to April
3. **Assembly and debugging** - May to June
4. **Testing and optimization** - July to August
5. **Competition sprint** - September to October
</Steps>

## Document Management

### Document categories

1. **Design documents** - technical solutions and calculation reports
2. **Meeting minutes** - weekly meetings and review records
3. **Training materials** - onboarding and technical-sharing decks
4. **Competition materials** - rules, registration files, and submissions
5. **Financial materials** - budget sheets, reimbursements, and accounts

### Storage strategy

<Aside type="tip">
Use cloud collaboration plus local backup. A practical combination is Feishu Docs for collaboration, a cloud drive for backup, and NAS storage for large files.
</Aside>

## Competition Registration

### Required submissions

| Item | Deadline | Owner |
|------|----------|-------|
| Registration form | June | Operations |
| Team-member list | June | Operations |
| Technical report | July | Technical groups |
| Cost report | July | Operations and finance |
| Design-presentation deck | August | Technical groups |

### Checklist

1. Follow official announcements closely
2. Prepare templates early
3. Reserve review and revision time
4. Use cross-checking by multiple members

## Team Building

### Recruitment workflow

<Steps>
1. Promotion campaign - posters and recruitment talks at the start of term
2. Resume collection - online forms
3. Interview screening - technical and general interviews
4. Team onboarding - safety and skills training
5. Trial assessment - a three-month evaluation period
</Steps>

### Team activities

- Technical sharing sessions every two weeks
- One or two team-building events each semester
- End-of-season summary meeting
- Recognition session for outstanding members

## Financial Management

### Budget planning

| Category | Share | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Materials | 40% | Steel, carbon fiber, and related materials |
| Outsourced manufacturing | 25% | CNC, 3D printing, and similar services |
| Electronic components | 15% | Sensors, controllers, and wiring hardware |
| Travel | 10% | Transportation and accommodation for competitions |
| Other | 10% | Tools, consumables, and miscellaneous items |

### Reimbursement process

1. Fill out the reimbursement form
2. Attach invoices or receipts
3. Department-head approval
4. Team-lead approval
5. Finance disbursement
