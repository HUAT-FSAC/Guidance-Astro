## Platform Mix

### Core channels

| Platform | Positioning | Update cadence |
|----------|-------------|----------------|
| WeChat Official Account | Official updates and long-form content | 1-2 posts per week |
| Douyin / Kuaishou | Short videos and behind-the-scenes clips | 2-3 posts per week |
| Bilibili | Technical sharing and vlogs | 2-4 videos per month |
| Xiaohongshu | Daily team life and recruitment content | 3-5 posts per week |

---

## Content Planning

### Content categories

1. **Competition coverage** - event highlights and result announcements
2. **Technical explainers** - race-car knowledge and engineering insights
3. **Team stories** - member interviews and workshop moments
4. **Recruitment promotion** - team introduction and role requirements
5. **Sponsor acknowledgements** - delivery of partnership benefits

### Content calendar

<Aside type="tip">
Use Notion or a Feishu multi-dimensional sheet to manage the content calendar and production pipeline.
</Aside>

## Video Production

### Recommended equipment

| Device | Suggested models | Purpose |
|--------|------------------|---------|
| Camera | Sony A7C / Fujifilm X-T5 | Main shooting setup |
| Action camera | GoPro 12 / DJI Action 4 | On-board footage |
| Drone | DJI Mini 4 Pro | Aerial shots |
| Gimbal | DJI RS3 | Moving shots |
| Microphone | RODE Wireless Go | Audio capture |

### Editing tools

- **Adobe Premiere Pro** - the professional default
- **DaVinci Resolve** - powerful free option
- **Jianying** - fast turnaround for short-form content

## Visual Standards

### Brand colors

| Color | Value | Use |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | #F39C12 | Headlines and emphasis |
| Secondary | #1A1A1A | Background surfaces |
| Accent | #FFFFFF | Body text and contrast |

### Design tools

- **Figma** - UI design and collaboration
- **Canva** - fast poster production
- **Adobe Illustrator** - vector graphics

## Analytics

### Key metrics

<Steps>
1. **Views and plays** - reach and exposure
2. **Engagement rate** - likes, comments, and shares
3. **Follower growth** - account health over time
4. **Conversion rate** - recruitment and sponsorship outcomes
</Steps>
