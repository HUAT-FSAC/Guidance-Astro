## Sponsor Tiers

### Sponsorship levels

| Tier | Contribution | Benefits |
|------|--------------|----------|
| Title Sponsor | 100,000 RMB+ | Naming rights on the car and full-channel exposure |
| Core Sponsor | 50,000-100,000 RMB | Car-body logo placement and event coverage |
| Strategic Partner | 20,000-50,000 RMB | Equipment support and featured promotion |
| Technical Support | 10,000-20,000 RMB | Product trials and technical exchange |
| In-kind Sponsor | Goods or services | Acknowledgement and showcase exposure |

---

## Outreach Workflow

<Steps>
1. **Target selection** - build a shortlist of potential sponsors
2. **Proposal drafting** - tailor sponsorship packages to each company
3. **Initial contact** - email, call, or visit the target organization
4. **Detailed discussion** - align needs and negotiate benefits
5. **Contract signing** - define responsibilities and deliverables
6. **Benefit delivery** - execute every promised exposure item on time
7. **Relationship maintenance** - provide regular updates and renewal follow-up
</Steps>

---

## Sponsorship Proposal

### Recommended structure

1. **Team profile** - competition history, scale, and capabilities
2. **Competition overview** - why FSAC matters and what it reaches
3. **Benefit list** - a concrete exposure and engagement package
4. **Past cases** - previous partners and observable outcomes
5. **Contact details** - clear owner and communication channel

<Aside type="tip">
The proposal should look polished and credible. It needs to communicate both the team’s capability and the seriousness of the partnership.
</Aside>

## Delivering Sponsor Benefits

### Exposure items

| Item | Delivery standard |
|------|-------------------|
| Car-body logo | Apply according to the contracted size and location |
| Team uniform logo | Place on chest, sleeve, or back as agreed |
| Event standee | Display on site during competitions and showcases |
| Official posts | Include sponsor exposure in scheduled articles |
| Video credits | Add sponsor acknowledgements in closing credits |

### Service items

- Technical report sharing
- Company visit and exchange sessions
- Campus-promotion support
- Product test feedback

---

## Relationship Management

### Routine communication

1. Holiday greetings
2. Competition-progress updates
3. Benefit-delivery feedback
4. Annual summary reports

### Renewal strategy

<Aside type="note">
Start renewal conversations two to three months before the season ends. Use delivered results and concrete outcomes to lock in next-season support early.
</Aside>
