## System overview

The traction battery is the energy source of the electric race car, so the design has to balance performance, safety, and cost.

<Aside type="danger">
High-voltage systems are a personal-safety risk. Follow safety procedures strictly.
</Aside>

---

## Cell selection

### Common cell options

| Model | Chemistry | Capacity | Discharge Rate | Unit Price |
|------|------|------|----------|------|
| 21700 | NCM | 5Ah | 3C | CNY 25 |
| 18650 | NCM | 3.5Ah | 2C | CNY 15 |
| Prismatic LFP | LFP | 50Ah | 2C | CNY 200 |

### Selection criteria

1. **Energy density** - affects endurance and usable energy
2. **Power density** - affects acceleration performance
3. **Cycle life** - affects long-term operating cost
4. **Safety** - especially thermal-runaway characteristics

## BMS system

### Functional modules

1. **Voltage acquisition** - per-cell voltage monitoring
2. **Temperature monitoring** - multi-point temperature sensing
3. **Balancing** - active or passive balancing
4. **SOC estimation** - coulomb counting plus open-circuit voltage
5. **Fault protection** - over-voltage, under-voltage, over-current, and over-temperature

### Recommended directions

- **In-house BMS** - typically based on STM32 plus an AFE chip
- **Commercial BMS** - off-the-shelf brands such as Lvdun or Gotech

---

## Thermal management

### Cooling approaches

| Method | Advantage | Drawback |
|------|------|------|
| Natural air cooling | simple | limited effect |
| Forced-air cooling | low cost | noisy |
| Liquid cooling | strong performance | heavier and more complex |

### Temperature targets

- normal operation: 25-45°C
- maximum allowed: 55°C
- low-temperature warm-up: above 0°C

---

## Safety design

### Required protection items

1. **Fuse** - short-circuit protection for the main loop
2. **Main relay** - high-voltage isolation
3. **Precharge circuit** - limits inrush current when closing the pack
4. **Insulation monitoring** - detects leakage paths
5. **Manual service disconnect (MSD)** - emergency isolation point

<Aside type="caution">
Battery-pack design must pass FSAC electrical inspection. Study the rules early rather than fixing issues late.
</Aside>
