## Overview

The localization and mapping group uses an integrated inertial navigation unit together with monocular cameras and LiDAR to:

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Read the Environment" icon="magnifier">
    Recognize the structure of the surrounding track and landmarks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Estimate Vehicle Pose" icon="star">
    Track the car position and heading in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build the Track Map" icon="document">
    Construct a consistent global map for downstream modules.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Support Planning" icon="random">
    Provide the spatial foundation used by planning and control.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Aside type="tip" title="Why It Matters">
  Precise localization is one of the foundations of autonomous driving. Without a reliable pose estimate, the car cannot plan or drive correctly.
</Aside>

---

## Technical Architecture

```
       IMU / GPS
           |
           v
 Integrated Navigation  <- high-rate pose estimate
           |
           v
 Visual / LiDAR SLAM    <- pose correction and mapping
           |
           v
      Global Map        <- consumed by planning
```
